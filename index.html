Som Sagar

Hi! I am a second-year computer science PhD student at Arizona State University University advised by Prof. Ransalu Senanayake and affiliated with the Laboratory for Learning Evaluation of autoNomous Systems (LENS). My research goal is to create robust and adaptable machine learning systems that are prepared for distribution changes and efficiently respond to new information. My work spans both supervised learning and reinforcement learning settings, with a focus on creating models that can generalize or adapt to changing environments.

Previously, I received a B.tech Honors. in computer scince from Indian Institute of Information Technology, Kottayam.

I am originally from Kerala, India, and outside of research, I enjoy spending time outdoors, especially playing soccer, hiking, and swimming.

Please feel free to reach out about research or any advice I can help with!
